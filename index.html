Intro

I'm a behavioral scientist who stumbled into product management, on a mission to make personalized coaching accessible to everyone.

You know that feeling when you buy a fancy new app and nobody uses it? I help companies solve what keeps their leaders up at night — getting employees to actually use the technology they've invested in.

At Ten Thousand Coffees, we took mentorship programs from "spreadsheet chaos" to "actually works at enterprise scale", growing from <$1M to $10M+ ARR. The numbers are exciting (1.5M+ mentorship connections across 50+ Fortune 500s), but what really makes me proud is knowing there's someone out there having a career-changing conversation right now because of something we built.

Outside of work, I'm throwing around weights, making memories with my soon-to-be wife , or practicing the gentle art of strangling friends (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu).
